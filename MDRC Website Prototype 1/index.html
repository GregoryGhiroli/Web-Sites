Bard

Bard is a general purpose ground robot that is being designed to fill many roles. It's immediate goal is entry into the Trinity Firefighting Competition in the Spring of 2012. However, the main goal of this robot is to become a platform that can be used extensively in MDRC for AI research, programming workshops, and many other tasks.
AMOS III
AMOS III is our main competition robot which has competed in MiniGrand and IGVC for the past several years.
Martlet
Entry for the 2009 Trinity Home Robot Firefighting Competition, designed around an Arduino and a circular base to be small and able to navigate hallways with ease.
Couch Bot
Couch Bot is a three-person carrying robot, that is designed to not only be the couch of mdrc, but the transport of heavier robot and club members to events around Rochester!
